More about our services

  • Diagnosis and Fault Finding

We analyze the symptoms and systematically identify the root cause of your vehicle’s problem. We utilize a variety of tools, including diagnostic software, sensors, multi-meters and visual inspections. Once the problem is identified, we recommend the best, most cost-effective solution.

  • Breakdowns/ Roadside Assistance

We provide on-site repairs or assistance to drivers who have experienced mechanical issues with their vehicles on the road. This can include jump-starting a dead battery, changing a flat tire, providing fuel, or diagnosing and fixing other mechanical issues that can be addressed on the spot. Our goal is to help drivers get their vehicles back on the road and safely to their intended destination.

  • Presale-inpections

Buying a new vehicle? Let one of our qualified technicians examine the vehicle first to avoid any nasty surprises later. Our mechanics perform a thorough assessment of the vehicle’s condition and identify any potential issues that may impact the safety, reliability, or value of the vehicle.
